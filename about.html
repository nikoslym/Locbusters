About

We work where production happens.

We were built from within the industry, by professionals who have spent years working on international and domestic productions.

Our focus is not on separating services, but on integrating them unit support, equipment rental, and location logistics operating as one coordinated system.

Our role is to ensure that every production is supported with clarity, precision, and reliability, so that decisions on the ground translate into a smoother, more controlled process.

From the first day of prep to the final day of wrap, we are the operational layer that keeps production moving quietly, reliably, and without friction.

Our Story

How we got here.

Founded in 2024 by four experienced film industry professionals, The Locbusters was created from within the production world.

Built on years of shared work across international and domestic productions in Greece, the founders had already collaborated extensively before forming the company.

Their experience spans independent films, television, and large-scale studio productions working in environments where time, pressure, and precision define every decision.

As Greece continues to grow as a global filming destination, the need for a reliable operational layer has become essential. The Locbusters was created to provide exactly that.

What brought the team together was a shared way of working: trust, respect, precision, and a deep understanding of how production operates on the ground.

Each founder continues to work within the industry independently, contributing specialised expertise across unit, production, and location departments. The company is a natural extension of this network.
